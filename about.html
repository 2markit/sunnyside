About Us

We opened our doors for the first time in 1980 on a 20 acre farm located in sunny Tsawwassen approx 20km outside of Vancouver, BC.

The two brothers, Len and Rene Van Vliet are the dedicated duo at Sunnyside Nursery. Len began Sunnyside with his parents, Jack and Jeanne over 30 years ago and just six years into the business, Rene joined in. They all worked hard and had an unfailing commitment to customer service. They have grown their nursery from a little wooden greenhouse to the large multi-greenhouse business that it is today.

The Van Vliet family has grown up in the Delta area, and now both Rene and Len live in Tsawwassen and are raising their families in this community. Both Len and Rene have studied horticulture and are ready to help you with any gardening queries. Come by our nursery, whether you are a beginner gardener, an expert or just browsing to find "that special something to fill a spot with colour", Rene and Len can easily be found at their store to help you find that certain plant you desire.

We are a family owned business with quality products and great service.  We encourage everyone who can to visit our garden center. In addition to our large and bright store area full of gardening supplies we have

  • an 8000 sq foot glass green house
  • 2 large hoop houses full of shade plants
  • 5 acres of outdoor retail space
  • delivery services for our plants and bulk material
  • experienced staff who can offer landscaping advice

Sunnyside Nurseries serves customers in both Canada and the United States.  All plants bound for the U.S. are certified by Agriculture Canada.  If you are located in the United States please contact us for more details.

We look forward to seeing you in our garden center and are eager to answer all of your gardening questions.
