Welcome to Sunnyside Nurseries!

South Delta’s most distinguished garden center.

Located in sunny Tsawwassen, Sunnyside is South Delta’s most distinguished garden center. Since 1980 we have been committed to helping our customers create their ideal garden space. We offer a wide variety of beautiful annuals and perennials as well as bulk materials, garden art, pottery, trees, and tools. We even offer delivery services for plants and bulk goods!


Whether you’re new to gardening or have a practiced green thumb Sunnyside is here to help you transform your garden into a space that you’re proud of. Our staff is dedicated to providing the highest level of customer care and are ready to answer your gardening questions!
