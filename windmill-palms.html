3 Steps for Stunning Windmill Palms

Where else in Canada can you grow striking, tropical palm trees other than right here in Vancouver?


The Windmill Palm (Trachycarpus fortunei) is a beautiful tree that lends your garden space a great tropical feel. Windmill palms are native to Asia, following these 3 steps YOU can grow stunning tropical trees in your space:

1. Sandy Aerated soil.

Palms prefer well drained soil with plenty of sand. Here in the lower mainland with so much rain this is especially important. Ensure that your garden soil is mixed with sand and well aerated before you plant.



2. Watering Habits.

Palms require very little water and too much can even be harmful. Make sure to water your palm sparingly.



3. Fertilize, Fertilize, Fertilize.

The more fertile the soil your palm is planted in the more productively it will grow. Feed your tree with a slow release granular fertilizer designed for palms to ensure the lushest growth on your palm.

Following these simple steps, you can grow lush tropical trees in your garden space! Visit us today to pick your perfect palm!
